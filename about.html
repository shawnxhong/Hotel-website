About Us

Welcome To
Myth Land Resort

Myth Land is a theme resort where lots of mythical creatures live and breed. There are log cabins located at the foot of the mountains, on the top of the mountains, at lake shores and in the middle of woods. You are encouraged to explore the mysterious forests area on the mountain as well as the tranquil lake, where you might be able to come across some incredible mythical creatures.
